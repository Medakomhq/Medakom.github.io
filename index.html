About Medakom

Medakom Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian-based manpower and outsourcing company delivering integrated workforce and human capital solutions across construction, plantation & reforestation, timber trading, logistics, manufacturing and cleaning services.

Founded with a mission to support Malaysia’s industrial and human capital growth, Medakom has earned a reputation for discipline, compliance, and reliability. With operations in Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah, and Sarawak, the company provides sustainable manpower services that align with both government regulations and international standards. We believe in long-term partnerships, ethical recruitment, and developing a workforce that supports Malaysia’s economic and environmental sustainability.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a trusted manpower and outsourcing partner — delivering reliable, compliant, and sustainable workforce solutions that strengthen industries and empower communities.

Mission

To provide ethical, efficient, and responsible human resource solutions that empower both people and businesses. Medakom integrates integrity, compliance, and human development to ensure a workforce ecosystem that grows sustainably for future generations.

Governance & Compliance

Medakom upholds strong governance standards in line with Malaysian labour and corporate laws:

  • Employment Act 1955
  • Private Employment Agencies Act 1981 (Akta 246)
  • Labour Ordinance (Sabah & Sarawak)
  • Occupational Safety and Health Act (DOSH)
  • HRD Corp Registered Training Provider
  • ESG (Environmental, Social & Governance) Compliance

We ensure every operation is transparent, ethical, and legally compliant, protecting the welfare and dignity of all employees under our care.
