About Medakom

We are a Malaysian manpower & outsourcing company supporting manufacturing, construction, plantation, logistics, and cleaning sectors across Peninsular, Sabah, and Sarawak.

  • Integrity & Compliance: Transparent operations aligned to Malaysian labour & immigration law.
  • Human Responsibility: Respect, safety and fair administration for all workers.
  • Sustainability: Long-term workforce stability & community impact.
  • Reliability: Contingency staffing & zero disruption service delivery.

Leadership

Our CEO brings over 30 years of hands-on experience in manpower, logistics, palm oil, and industrial management. He upholds discipline, accountability, and workforce welfare as the foundation of Medakom’s growth.

Director — Mrs. Melawati (Shella Chong)

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a trusted HR & manpower partner delivering integrated and sustainable workforce solutions across industries.

Mission: To empower people and businesses through ethical, reliable and efficient workforce management.

Services & Functions

  • Outsourcing & Logistics Management
  • Timber Trading & Construction (G7)
  • Palm Oil & Reforestation Management
  • Cleaning & Manufacturing Workforce Support

Governance & Compliance
