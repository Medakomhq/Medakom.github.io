About Medakom

Medakom Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian-based manpower and outsourcing company delivering integrated workforce and human capital solutions across multiple industries — including construction, plantation, timber trading, logistics, manufacturing, and cleaning services.

Founded with a mission to support Malaysia’s industrial and human capital growth, Medakom has earned a reputation for discipline, compliance, and reliability. With operations in Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah, and Sarawak, the company provides sustainable manpower services that align with both government regulations and international standards.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a trusted manpower and outsourcing partner — delivering reliable, compliant, and sustainable workforce solutions that strengthen industries and empower communities.

Mission

To provide ethical, efficient, and responsible human resource solutions that empower both people and businesses — integrating integrity, compliance, and human development to build a sustainable workforce ecosystem for future generations.
