About Us

Medakom Sdn. Bhd. is a professional manpower supplier and outsourcing company providing complete workforce solutions across Malaysia’s major industries including manufacturing, logistics, timber trading, construction, plantation, and forestry management.

Leadership

With over two decades of experience in HR contracting and workforce management, Mr. Chong leads Medakom with integrity, professionalism, and a vision for sustainable growth.

Mrs. Melawati (Shella Chong) – Director

Mrs. Melawati oversees company operations and compliance, ensuring Medakom’s continued excellence in manpower supply and human capital development.
