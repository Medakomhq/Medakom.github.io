About Us

Medakom Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian manpower supplier and outsourcing company providing integrated workforce solutions across manufacturing, logistics, timber trading, construction, palm oil, cleaning and forestry / reforestation management sectors.

We focus on compliance, structured deployment, operational discipline, and human capital sustainability. Our role is to help clients run operations while we manage the workforce ethically and professionally.

We take pride in being one of Malaysia’s trusted providers of specialized manpower and outsourcing services. We are committed to supporting industries that depend heavily on foreign and local workers by providing ethical, reliable, and cost-effective workforce solutions.
