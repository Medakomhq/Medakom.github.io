About Medakom

Medakom Sdn. Bhd. is a licensed manpower supply and outsourcing company providing integrated HR, logistics, and sustainable workforce solutions across Malaysia’s key industries.

Integrity & Compliance — We uphold transparency, fairness, and adherence to all Malaysian labour laws.

Human Responsibility — We respect every worker, ensuring safe and ethical employment.

Sustainability — We build long-term workforce ecosystems with compliance and care.

Reliability — We maintain operational excellence and client trust through discipline and dedication.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be Malaysia’s trusted partner in ethical and sustainable workforce management.

Mission: To deliver responsible manpower, HR contracting, and industry support solutions that empower people, strengthen businesses, and sustain communities.

Governance & Compliance

Medakom operates under strict governance, approved by the Ministry of Home Affairs (KDN) and the Department of Labour (JTKSM). Our company maintains transparent documentation, contracts, and operational procedures to ensure compliance with all legal and ethical standards for manpower and outsourcing operations.
