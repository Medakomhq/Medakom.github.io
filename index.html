About Us

Medakom Sdn. Bhd. is a professional manpower supplier and outsourcing company providing integrated workforce solutions to manufacturing, construction, logistics, palm oil, timber trading, and forestry/reforestation sectors in Malaysia. We focus on compliance, structured deployment, cost efficiency, and human capital sustainability.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a trusted manpower and outsourcing company delivering integrated, ethical and sustainable workforce solutions across Malaysia’s key industries.

Mission: To provide dependable labour solutions, structured deployment, compliance-based management, and long-term partnership value — empowering both people and business growth.

Core Values

  • Integrity & Compliance — We operate with transparency and follow Malaysian labour and industry standards.
  • Human Responsibility — We believe that every worker deserves respect, safety, proper treatment and fair administration.
  • Sustainability — We focus on long-term workforce stability, not just short-term placement.
  • Reliability — We deliver manpower solutions that help clients keep operations running without disruption.
  • Partnership Mindset — We build relationships, not just contracts.

Key Strength

  • Deep experience with industries that depend on high-volume labour, including plantation, cleaning, factory, construction, logistics, and timber.
  • Ability to manage both local and foreign workers with documentation, supervision, and site control.
  • Hands-on operations model — not just agency paperwork; we manage people on-site.
  • Cost-effective solutions that help clients reduce overhead while keeping output stable.
  • Strong network in Sabah, Sarawak, and Peninsular Malaysia.
