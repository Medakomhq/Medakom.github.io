About Medakom Sdn. Bhd.

A registered and licensed manpower outsourcing, construction, and trading company established to support Malaysia’s key industries with integrity and sustainability.

Our operations cover manpower supply, HR contracting, logistics, timber trading, construction (G7), cleaning, and plantation services across Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah, and Sarawak.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be Malaysia’s trusted integrated manpower management partner delivering sustainable workforce and business growth.

Mission: We empower people and industries through ethical practices, innovation, and long-term collaboration.

Governance & Compliance

Medakom operates in strict compliance with the Ministry of Home Affairs (KDN), Ministry of Human Resources (MOHR), and Malaysian labour laws. All recruitment, housing, and safety processes follow ethical and regulatory standards to protect both employees and clients.
