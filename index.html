About Us

Medakom Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian manpower supplier and outsourcing company offering integrated solutions in manufacturing, construction, logistics, timber trading, palm oil, and forestry management sectors.

Leadership

CEO’s Message — Mr. Andrew Chong (Aidil Chong)

In Medakom, we take pride in being a leading provider of specialized HR services in Malaysia, dedicated to addressing the unique challenges of sectors that rely heavily on foreign workers. Understanding the complexities of 4D jobs, we are committed to delivering expert HR contracting services that help businesses optimize costs while meeting their staffing needs effectively.

Our company has built a strong reputation for professionalism and reliability, bringing together a talented team of HR professionals, service delivery experts, and consultants. We operate with a human-oriented approach, placing customer needs at the heart of everything we do, and continuously striving for mutually beneficial outcomes that help our clients grow and thrive.
