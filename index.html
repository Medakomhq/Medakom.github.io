About Medakom

We are a Malaysian manpower & outsourcing company supporting manufacturing, construction, plantation, logistics, and cleaning sectors across Peninsular, Sabah, and Sarawak.

What We Do (Functions)

  • Manpower Supply: recruit, mobilise, place and manage local/foreign workers to client sites.
  • HR Contracting: payroll, attendance, levy/PERKESO/EIS, hostel & transportation arrangements.
  • Operational Support: on-site supervision, shift coverage, productivity monitoring.
  • Plantation & Forestry: palm oil field teams, reforestation crew, safety & compliance on estates.
  • Logistics & Shipment: barge/lorry coordination, loading/unloading, yard & scrap handling.
  • Cleaning (G7): industrial cleaning, scheduled housekeeping & post-construction cleanup.

Core Values

  • Integrity & Compliance: transparent processes aligned to Malaysian labour & immigration law.
  • Human Responsibility: dignity, safety, proper documentation and fair administration.
  • Sustainability: long-term workforce stability & community impact.
  • Reliability: zero-disruption mindset, contingency staffing & on-call support.
  • Partnership: we grow with clients through long-term contracts not one-off jobs.
