About Medakom

Medakom Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian-based manpower and outsourcing company providing integrated workforce and human capital management solutions across multiple industries — including construction, plantation, timber trading, logistics, manufacturing, and cleaning services.

Founded with a mission to support Malaysia’s industrial and human capital growth, Medakom has earned a reputation for discipline, compliance, and reliability. With operations in Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah, and Sarawak, the company provides sustainable manpower services that align with government regulations and international standards.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a trusted manpower and outsourcing partner — delivering reliable, compliant, and sustainable workforce solutions that strengthen industries and empower communities.

Mission: To provide ethical, efficient, and responsible human resource solutions that empower both people and businesses, while ensuring a workforce ecosystem that grows sustainably for future generations.

Governance & Compliance

Medakom upholds strong governance standards under Malaysian labour laws:

  • Employment Act 1955
  • Private Employment Agencies Act 1981 (Akta 246)
  • Labour Ordinance (Sabah & Sarawak)
  • Occupational Safety and Health Act (DOSH)
  • HRD Corp Registered Training Provider
  • ESG (Environmental, Social & Governance) Compliance
