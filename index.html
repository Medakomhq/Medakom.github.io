Core Values

Integrity & Compliance — We operate with transparency and follow Malaysian labour and industry standards.

Human Responsibility — We believe every worker deserves respect, safety, fair administration, and dignity at work.

Sustainability — We focus on long-term workforce stability, not just short-term placement.

Reliability — We deliver manpower solutions that keep client operations running without disruption.

Partnership Mindset — We build relationships, not just contracts.

Key Strengths

Deep experience in industries with high labour demand: plantation, palm oil, timber, cleaning, factory, construction, and logistics.

We manage both local and foreign workers, including documentation, supervision, discipline, and payroll compliance.

Hands-on operating model — not just agency paperwork, we run and supervise people on-site.

Cost-effective solutions to help clients reduce overhead while keeping full compliance.

Leadership
CEO’s Message — Mr. Andrew Chong (Aidil Chong)
In Medakom, we take pride in being one of Malaysia’s trusted providers of specialized manpower and outsourcing services. We are committed to supporting industries that depend heavily on both foreign and local workers by providing ethical, reliable, and cost-effective workforce solutions.
Our company has built a strong reputation for professionalism and reliability, backed by a talented team of HR experts, service consultants, and field specialists. We operate with a human-oriented approach — placing our clients’ needs at the heart of everything we do while continuously striving for mutually beneficial outcomes.
As we continue to grow, we are supported by our group companies — Era Holistic Ventures Sdn. Bhd. and Active Forest Solutions Sdn. Bhd. — delivering excellence in HR and reforestation management. Together, we aim to create long-term value for clients and contribute to sustainable development across Malaysia.
